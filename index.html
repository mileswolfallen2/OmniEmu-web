Everything You Need

A complete solution for managing your retro gaming library

One-Click Setup

Download, install, and auto-configure 20+ emulators from inside the app. No terminal required.

ROM Library

Scan your ROMs, browse by platform, and launch games directly from a unified library.

Game Art Scraping

Automatically fetches covers, screenshots, and titles for your entire collection.

Controller Navigation

Navigate the entire UI with a gamepad using DPad, A, B, and Start buttons.

BIOS Manager

Scan your BIOS files and update RetroArch's config in one click.

Cross-Platform

Identical experience on Windows, macOS, and Linux (x64 & ARM64).

Cloud Sync Beta

Sync your game saves between devices with built-in Syncthing integration. Free and fully local.

Decomp Projects Beta

Install and manage native PC ports built from reverse-engineered source code.

Get Started in Seconds

From download to playing in four simple steps

1

Download

Grab the latest release for your platform from the downloads page.

2

Install Emulators

Open OmniEmu, go to Library, and install your favorite emulators with one click.

3

Scan ROMs

Point OmniEmu to your ROMs directory and let it build your library automatically.

4

Play

Browse your collection, select a game, and launch it instantly.
